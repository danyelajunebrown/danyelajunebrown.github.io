# Product Context

## Purpose
This application helps users track their emotional states and coping skills for DBT therapy. The diary card feature allows users to visualize their emotional patterns over a week.

## User Experience Goals
- Simple, intuitive feelings selection via spinning wheel
- Clear visual feedback for intensity and skills
- Easy-to-use weekly diary card with drag-and-drop
- Reliable PDF export for sharing with therapists

## Current Pain Points
- PDF generation has multiple issues:
  1. "Generating PDF" message appears on output
  2. Diary card content is truncated (not full content captured)
  3. Content is shifted left with empty space on right
  4. Excessive empty space on bottom of PDF

## Success Criteria
- Full diary card content captured in PDF
- Content centered on page with proper margins
- No UI artifacts in output
- Works with varying amounts of diary entries
